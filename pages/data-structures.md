## What is a data structure?

> A structure where you can store, arrange, and manage your data, _depending on the use case_

Choosing a data structure depends on

- How you want to store the data (linear, non-linear, etc.)
  - Do I only want to store unique data?
  - Do I need to keep my data sorted all the time?
  - Can I store my data in the order they were inserted?
- How you want to access your data (indexes, keys, sequential, direct)
